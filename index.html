About Mbogo College

Founded in Kampala, Mbogo College School is built on the values of discipline, knowledge, and community. We offer both O-Level and A-Level curricula (Uganda National Examinations Board) with a focus on science, arts, and technology.

Mission

To nurture holistic individuals equipped for higher education and life.

Vision

To be a centre of academic excellence in East Africa.
