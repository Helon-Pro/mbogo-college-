About Mbogo College School

Founded in 2005, Mbogo College School is a leading secondary school in Kampala, Uganda. We offer both O-Level and A-Level curricula following the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) standards.

Our Mission

To nurture holistic individuals equipped for higher education and life through quality teaching, discipline, and innovation.

Our Vision

To be the centre of academic excellence in East Africa, producing globally competitive graduates.

Core Values

Discipline • Integrity • Excellence • Innovation • Community • Respect
